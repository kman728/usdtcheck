Common Questions

What is BNB Smart Chain (BSC)?

A blockchain for smart contracts, parallel to Binance Chain. Offers EVM compatibility, low fees, and high performance for DApps.

How do I verify an asset?

Input its contract address into a blockchain explorer (e.g., BscScan) to check details. Our tool (when functional) streamlines this.

Which wallets support BSC?

MetaMask, Trust Wallet, MathWallet, and hardware wallets (Ledger, Trezor) are commonly used. Ensure correct BSC network configuration.
